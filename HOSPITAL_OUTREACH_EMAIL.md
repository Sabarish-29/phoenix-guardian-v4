## Common Objections & Responses

**"We don't work with student projects"**
"I understand. We're approaching this professionally with comprehensive documentation and compliance frameworks. Would you be willing to review our materials and reconsider? If not, could you recommend someone who might be interested in healthcare innovation partnerships?"

**"We don't have bandwidth right now"**
"I completely understand. Would it be appropriate to follow up in 3-6 months? In the meantime, I'd be happy to share our progress via email."

**"Our legal/compliance team would never approve this"**
"That's a valid concern. We've prepared comprehensive compliance documentation (HIPAA, FDA, security policies) specifically to address these concerns. Would your compliance team be willing to review our materials? We're committed to meeting all requirements."

**"How is this different from existing solutions?"**
"Great question. We're not trying to replace your EHR. We're offering supplemental AI assistance for documentation. What makes us different is our transparency — all code is open source, all metrics are real, and we're honest about our limitations. We need clinical validation, which is why we're seeking a partner."

**"What's your business model?"**
"Right now, we're focused on validation, not revenue. This pilot would be completely free. Our goal is to prove the technology works in a real clinical setting. Future monetization would likely be SaaS-based, but that's not our immediate focus."
